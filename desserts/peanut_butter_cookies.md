Peanut Butter Cookies
=====================

* 1 cup brown sugar
* 1 cup white sugar
* 1 cup peanut butter
* 1 cup butter
* 2 eggs
* 2.5 cups sifted flour
* 1/2 tsp baking soda
* 1 tsp baking powder

Mix ingredients and shape into small balls. Put on greased cookie sheet and press down with a fork. Bake at 350 for 12 minutes.

Makes 6 dozen.
